What is a service?

  • Services are singletons that carry out specific tasks.
  • You can use Angular existant services, which provide support for common web apps needs ($http, $location, etc.)...
  • Or you can ( and you should ) create your own services, which will handle your own application logic.
  • Remember that controllers should not contain any business logic, and that they will be remove from AngularJs 2 and so that in a new application, services should really contain most of your logic
angular.module('sample') //Let's inject our userService service .controller('DirectiveCtrl', ['$scope', '$http', 'userService', function($scope, $http, userService){ $http({method: 'GET', url: '/users'}). success(function(data, status, headers, config) { $scope.users = data; }); $scope.deleteUser = function(user) { userService.delete(user); }; }]);
