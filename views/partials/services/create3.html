About providers

  • Services DI works as followed:
    1. A controller/directive/service/etc. need a service named UserService...
    2. Angular will search for the provider called UserServiceProvider in the provider cache...
    3. It calls its method $get to create UserService instance...
    4. And stores it in the services cache, for further use (services are singletons).
  • There are several types of services, and most of time, you will not have to define a provider for your service. In such a case, it will be created under the hood by Angular.
  • Providers have the same name than the services prefixed by Provider, and are usefull when you need to configure your service...
