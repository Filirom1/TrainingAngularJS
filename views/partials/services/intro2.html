How to use a service?

  • To make the service available in your code, you have to:
    • Be sure that your module has a dependency to the module which owns this service
    • Make the service available in your controller/directive/other service by injecting it
  • The array notation allows to identify services in case you minify your js files, it's a good practice to use it
  • You can also use ng-annotate which will add dependency injection annotations for you
angular.module('sample',[]) .controller('myController', ['$scope', 'myService', function($scope, myService){ $scope.myVar= myService.doJob(); }]);
