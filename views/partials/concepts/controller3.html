Linking controllers to views/templates

  • You can associate your controllers via:
  • The ngController directive.
  • The ngRoute service associate a route to views and controllers
  • Remark: A new module will be available soon (angular 1.4) for routing: ngNewRouter. Have a look here for more details
<div ng-controller="ContactController"> ... </div> angular.module('trainingApp', ['ngRoute']) .config(function ($routeProvider) { $routeProvider .when('/home', { templateUrl: 'views/main.html', controller: MainController }) .when('/contact', { templateUrl: 'views/contact.html', }) .otherwise({ redirectTo: '/home' }); });
